About

About Olympiad '17

Spanning over four days; consisting of more than 30 events and 3 social events, NUST Olympiad 2016 is going to be exactly what it aspires to be: exceedingly phenomenal! Offering different activities, NUST Olympiad is going to set the benchmark for future events, all over Pakistan! So, what’s holding you back? Be prepared for the biggest event of the year, from 5th to 8th May. Make amazing friends and of course, enjoy your time at NUST Olympiad 2016!
