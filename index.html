About Olympiad '17

Every person has a unique set of skills that deserve to be showcased, admired and even rewarded. This belief forms the basis for NUST Olympiad 2017, where different societies of NUST will collaborate to deliver one of the leading events in Pakistan. Olympiad, spanning over four days, will comprise of 30+ versatile events to accommodate everyone, 3 social events to add just the right spin and thrill to your competitive days and finally, an unparalleled welcome, cordiality and a wide, unrestrained space to explore your potential and your surroundings.
