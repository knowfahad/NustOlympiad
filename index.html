About Olympiad '17

Winter is finally here, and with it, we are bringing to you NUST Olympiad 2017. NUST and Islamabad, with their infinite beauty and charm are ready to host you at a magnificent event, comprising of 30 events to accommodate everyone, 3 social events to add just the right spin and thrill to your competitive days and finally, an unparalleled welcome, cordiality and opportunity for openness to explore your potential and your surroundings. Begin your journey with us by exploring virtually, for starters, and finding the right fit for you here at NUST Olympiad.
