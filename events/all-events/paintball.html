Paintball

Procedure
  • This game comprises three rounds, each with high difficulty level.
  • Each team (of three individuals) will be given a small basket of images, Board, thumb pins and sample sheets.
  • They have to paste images on pin boards in the similar fashion as in template. ( The images will be in pairs, with one image visible on the board and others are supposed to be pasted)
  • In first ROUND 16 Images, 2nd ROUND 26 and final round 60 images target will be given.
Rules And Regulations
  • Template sheet will be taken back.
  • Teams have to memorize the sequence of images.
  • No one can take picture of template sheet.
  • Time will be same for each team.
Timings
  • 1st round:
    Template will be displayed for 1 min and the participants will be given 30 sec to complete the picture.
  • 2nd Round:
    Template will be displayed for 2 min and the participants will be given 1 min to complete the picture.
  • 3rd Round:
    Template will be displayed for 3 min and the participants will be given 1 min 30 sec to complete the picture.
Judgement Criteria
  • The participants to get the maximum pairs right in the allocated time wins!
