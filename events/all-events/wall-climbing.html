Wall Climbing

  • Time will start with whistle and stop when climber touches the bell.
  • Only two false starts will be allowed.
  • Average time of two referees will be noted down.
  • Body weight of climber on the rope will disqualify him.
  • Only holds will be used while climbing.
  • Holding wall sides or rope will disqualify the climber.
  • Second chance will be given to only those participant who faced technical faults (Will be decide by judges).
