Individual Competitions

Round 1 : Nose Dive

Qualification:
  • Transport at least 10 cotton balls for successful qualification.
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points.
  • Each extra ball delivered gives 1 bonus point.
Instructions:
  • Set up the bowls 12′ apart, with the cotton balls placed in 1 bowl.
  • When the clock starts, contestant may dip nose in petroleum jelly and attempt to pick up cotton balls with the nose.
  • Player may only apply petroleum jelly by dipping nose in petroleum jelly.
  • If player transports more than 1 cotton ball at a time, any cotton balls that are deposited in the end bowl will not count towards the total.
  • Player must deposit cotton ball into the end bowl directly from the nose without coming into contact with any other body part or object.
  • To complete the game, player must transport 5 cotton balls from the start bowl to the end bowl, with all 5 cotton balls in the bowl concurrently with the 60-second time limit.

Round 2 : Movin’ On Up

Qualification:
  • Successful completion of the challenge
Instructions:
  • Set up the stack of cups so that there’s 1 red cup with 39 blue cups stacked on top of it.
  • When the clock starts, player may grab a single cup from the top of the stack and place it on the bottom of the stack.
  • Player must use 1 hand to grab each cup, and must alternate hands with each cup.
  • If more than 1 cup is removed at a time, player must place all cups that are outside of the stack back on top of the stack before continuing.
  • Player must play the game from a standing position.
  • To complete the game, the red cup must be placed on the bottom after traveling through the stack, and the stack must be held in 1 hand at the red cup within the 60-second time limit.

Round 3 : Rapid Fire

Qualification:
  • Knock atleast 2 cups off the table using rubber bands for successful qualification.
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points.
  • Each extra cup knocked off gives 1 bonus point.
Instructions:
  • Set up 6 empty disposable cups in a triangle formation on top of a table.
  • Place foul line 8′ away.
  • When the clock starts, player must stay behind the foul line and shoot 1 rubber band at a time at the cups.
  • To complete the game, player knock off 2 cans only by shooting rubber bands within the 60-second time limit.

Round 4 : Match Maker

Qualification:
  • Gather atleast 10 candies for successful qualification.
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points.
  • Each extra candy gathered gives 1 bonus point.
Instructions:
  • Randomly place each candy beneath an overturned cup at the center of the playing area.
  • Place each of the 3 glasses approximately 8′ away, each in a different direction from the center cups.
  • When the clock starts, player may lift the first cup and must then pick up the candy underneath and place it into 1 of the glasses.
  • If a player overturns more than 1 cup at a time, the game is over.
  • Small candies must be placed by color in each glass on each podium. Player may choose which glass contains which color.
  • If a player places candies of different colors in the same glass, the game is over.
  • To complete the game, the contestant must place atleast 10 candies into the glasses within the 60-second time limit.

Round 5 : Cap N’ Carry

Qualification:
  • Deliver atleast 8 tooth brushes for successful qualification.
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points.
  • Each extra toothbrush delivered gives 1 bonus point.
Instructions:
  • Each participant will have to pick up one toothbrush at a time, from a glass, with the peak of a cap he/she is wearing.
  • Handling the toothbrush or supporting it with any other part of the body while taking it to the bowl placed at the distant table will result disqualification.
  • Participant will have to place the toothbrush in another glass placed 8’ apart from the first glass.

Round 6 : Puddle Jumper.

Instructions:
  • 2 rows of 6 disposable glasses filled with water will be set up on the table a few inches apart.
  • The participant will have to place a ping pong ball in a glass and blow the ball into the adjacent glass in the other row.
  • Participant with maximum number of ball jumped from one glass to the other will win the round.
