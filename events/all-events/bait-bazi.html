Bait Bazi

  • Each team will comprise of up to 2 members.
  • Two teams will go head to head.
  • A coin toss will decide which team gets to go first.
  • The first team may recite from the alphabet given by the judge The second team will follow up with a stanza that starts from the letter the other team ended on.
  • Teams must respond within a 45 second of the other team’s end of verse.
  • Each round will be of 10 mins If during this time a team fails to respond within the given time, the other team will be declared the winner immediately.
  • Event Name “Bait Bazi” Objective Participating teams will go head to head in a contest of poetry.
  • Contestants will have to display spontaneity in coming up with original and inspired verses. Target Audience Any and all who can appreciate poetry Participants per Team 2 Max.
  • Teams 16 Time Limit 3 hrs Rounds / Phases 3 Judging Criterion Quality of poetry.
  • Requirements Quick wits Headed by Location SCME Seminar Hall.
  • Otherwise, upon completion of a round the victor shall be decided by our panel of judges based on the quality of verses produced by each team.
  • A piece of poetry must contain at least 2 verses before it is deemed acceptable.
