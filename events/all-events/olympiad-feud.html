Olympiad Feud

Olympiad Feud is a quiz competition in which 2 teams consisting of 3-5 people will compete at a time. The game has 4 rounds. Each round further consists of 5 questions. There will be a total of 20 questions for one game. Questions of a specific round is constrained to specific theme such as general knowledge, environmental issues, history ,mathematics ,culture ,technology and etc.

  • The first two rounds will be elimination rounds in which 1 team out of 2 competing at a time, will qualify for round 3 and 4. There will be a “Question of fortune” in round 3 and 4 which enables a team to get 10 additional points. A total of 50 points will be there.
  • Scoring criteria is more interesting in this game. Each right answer will give team, points corresponding to the round number (Correct answer in round 2 will give you 2 points). The team with maximum score will be winner.
Rules
  • There will be total of three rounds.
  • Round one and two will be buzzer rounds with elimination. If a team is unable to answer correctly, the question will be passed on to the team that buzzes first.
  • Final round will involve negative marking for wrong answers.
