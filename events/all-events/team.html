Team Competitions | Minute to Win it


Team Event

Participants per Team: 1-3
Event Fee: Rs. 500
Winning Prize: Rs. 8,000
Runner up's Prize: Rs. 3,000

Round 1 : The Guide Game

Qualification:
  • Gather atleast 5 objects and place them in the basket placed at the starting point for successful qualification
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points
  • Each extra object gathered gives 1 bonus point.
Instructions:
  • An empty basket will be placed at the starting point.
  • Player one will be blindfolded and in 60 secs player 2 has to guide him to collect each object.
  • The team will complete the round successfully on collecting atleast 5 objects and placing them in the basket placed at the starting point.

Round 2 : Don’t Panic

Qualification:
  • Deliver atleast 6 balls for successful qualification
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points
  • Each extra ball delivered gives 1 bonus point.
Instructions:
  • Raise a pingpong ball and each member will stand in a row.
  • Each member will be given a cup to catch the ball.
  • The team successfully passing atleast 6 balls through the row without dropping qualifies.

Round 3: Throw N’ Catch

Qualification:
  • Basket atleast 5 balls for successful qualification.
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points.
  • Each extra ball thrown successfully in the basket gives 1 bonus point.
Instructions:
  • Adjust a basket on one player’s head and keep the other player at a distance.
  • In 60 seconds player 2 will have to throw pingpong balls into the basket on his head.
  • Player 1 with the basket cannot take a step or sway his head.
  • The team with the most balls in their basket wins.

Round 4 : Blind Serve

Qualification:
  • Catch atleast 6 balls in the cup for successful qualification
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points
  • Each extra ball caught successfully in the cup gives 1 bonus point.
Instructions:
  • In this challenge, all three members of the team will participate.
  • One member will be blindfolded and will have to serve ping pong balls using a table tennis racket in his/her hand.
  • The other 2 members will be holding a cup and will be standing 6’ away from the first member such that they are facing the back of the first.
  • These 2 members will not be able to take a step out of their position and will have to catch the ping pong balls served by the first members only using the disposable cup in their hand.
  • If the two members catching the balls take a step out of their positions, the team will lose.
  • The team able to catch more number of balls will win the round.

Round 5 : Speed Eraser

Qualification:
  • Both team members should bounce and place atleast 2 pencils in the cups at the same time for successful qualification
  • Successful qualification secures 10 points
  • Each extra pencil bumped and placed into the cup successfully gives 3 bonus points.
Instructions:
  • In this challenge, 2 members from a team will participate.
  • Both members will attempt to bounce a pencil into disposable glass.
  • This will have to be repeated 7 times by both the members simultaneously. Failure to bounce 2 pencils into 2 glasses arranged in a row by any member will result in elimination.
