Make it Right


Team Event

Participants per Team: 5
Event Fee: Rs. 4,000
Winning Prize: Rs. 20,000
Runner up's Prize: Rs. 10,000

Objective

The main objective includes problem identification and extending it to offer solutions for those problems. The program also includes integrated planning framework and to infuse a strong dose of real-world ills.

Description

The participants will be given scenarios from real world issues and will be expected to find out the problems in those scenarios. This will reflect their problem identification capabilities, whereas in the next stages different scenarios will be given and they would have to find out the best solutions for the problems identified by them.

Audience

Students of all disciplines interested in Business
