NUST Youth Parliament


Individual Event

Event Fee: Rs. 400
Winning Prize: Rs. 6,000
Runner up's Prize: Rs. 3,000

Description
  • It will be simulation of the Pakistan National Assembly.
  • It will be a two-day conference with 6 committee sessions, 3 on each day with breaks in between.
  • Each participant will be assigned a political personality and he/she will have to represent it throughout the committee sessions.
Rules
  • The standard rules of Model United Nation Conferences will be observed in the committee sessions.
  • Two topics and guide booklets on the topics will be given to the delegates prior to the sessions.
  • At the end of the sessions and debate, the delegates will come up with a draft resolution which will address the possible solutions for the issues under discussion.
  • The sessions will be moderated and judged by a speaker and two deputy speakers. The speakers and deputy speakers will be seasoned PNA chairs of the MUN circuit.
