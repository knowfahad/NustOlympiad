The Crimeline Road


Team Event


Participants per Team: 2-3
Event Fee: Rs. 1,500
Winning Prize: Rs. 8,000
Runner up's Prize: Rs. 4,000


Rules and Regulations
  • Teams will consist of 2-3 members.
  • The whole debacle is a timed investigation, with every team being provided equal and ample amount of time for different clues.
  • Following the time constraints is a must.
  • Failure to do so may lead to disqualification.
  • The decisions of ushers on matters during the event are final and to be respected.
  • Damaging anything belonging to the building would lead to strict action.
  • Questions related to the investigation are to be asked only from the specified executives.
  • The investigation is to be carried out only in the specified area inside or outside the building.
  • Participants may not stroll to another place without informing the executives.
  • The participants will be given sufficient time (at least 10 hours) to write their report.
  • The judgement criteria will be intimated on the event day.
