Pakistan Got Talent

  • Time limit for each performance would be 10 minutes (max).
  • Each extra second will result in 0.5 marks penalty.
  • Foul language is not allowed and would result in disqualification.
  • Hate speech, targeting someone and inappropriate actions are not allowed.
  • The participants have to bring their own instruments.
  • Solo performances are also involved.
  • For team performances minimum number of team members should be 3 and maximum of 5 members.
  • Maximum of 32 teams can take part in the whole event.
  • Complete set of lights and sound system will be provided by Olympiad team.
  • For group participations, they would be also be judged on the basis of synchronization with other team members.
  • The teams will arrange their own props, costumes and makeup. Limited props will be provided on request depending on the availability.
