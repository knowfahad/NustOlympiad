Who wants to be a Millionaire

  • All the applicants will go through a primary qualification round, similar to the hot seat round, where they will be asked general IQ questions.
  • 8-10 contestants who score the highest will make it to the final hot seat round.
  • Questions will be of general knowledge and IQ.
  • Each question will have four options, and only one from them will be correct.
  • Each correct answer will get the candidate a specific point.
  • The contestant having the highest points in the end of the hot seat round will win.
  • On giving a wrong answer, game over.
  • The winner, and runner up will be given prize money and gift hampers.
  • A contestant will be given total of 4 lives which can help them in answering the questions. They will be:
    • Audience pool
    • Call a friend
    • 50-50
    • Change the question
  • Each of these lives can be used only once by each contestant.
