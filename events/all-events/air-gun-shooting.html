Air-Gun Shooting

  • Each participant will be allowed a total of 15 shots which are broken down as:
    1. Practice round 5 shots
    2. Round 1 5 shots
    3. Round 2 5 shots Total 15 shots
  • The participants will shoot from a distance of 20m.
  • The practice round will help them familiarize with the equipment with the help of a trainer.
  • The shots fired during this round will not be scored.
  • The competition consists of two rounds in which the participants will fire a total of 10 shots and the person with the maximum points wins the event.
  • In case of a stalemate, the participants will play another round in which they will fire shots from a distance of 35m.
  • The highest scoring participant wins.
