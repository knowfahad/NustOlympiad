Mathletics

Round 1: Sphinx

The first round will be a basic test of problems related to mathematics. Each team (2-3 members) will be given a question sheet comprising of objective and subjective questions. The teams must bring their calculators to solve the questions.


Rules
  • Teams will be given 60 minutes to solve to question sheet.Anyone found using unfair means to win the round, will be disqualified.
  • The question sheet will contain the marking criteria and relevant instructions.

Round 2: Saw

In this round, teams will be given several puzzles to solve. It will be a race against time so the teams which correctly solves their puzzles in the least time, gets the maximum score. The puzzles will be decided later by the module head but would generally be of the type Soduko and KenKen.


Rules
  • Teams will be given appropriate time to solve each puzzle.
  • Solution of each puzzle will need to achieve a certain pre-set criteria in order to move forward to the next one;for example 90% correct Sudoku, 80% correct KenKen, 70% correct Magic Square.
  • Only teams meeting criteria will move forward.
  • No electronic gadgets (calculator/mobile phones) are permitted in this round.
