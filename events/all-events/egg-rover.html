The Egg Rover Mission

This module is designed to test the engineering and aesthetic sense of the participants. With limited resources and time, they will be expected to complete a challenging task that will be dropping an egg from a certain height. The catch being, the safe landing of the egg on the ground. Points will be given on the innovation of design and the egg’s condition while it hits the ground.

Rules
  • The material will be revealed and provided on the event day and will have no prerequisites.
  • Participants would not be allowed to use their cell phones, laptops or any other electronic gadgets. Any team found taking help from the internet or any outside source will be disqualified.
  • Use of any material not provided by Science Bee team will not be allowed.
  • Using scissors with utmost care is important.
  • Following the time limit for each task is imperative.
  • Teams not adhering to the time limit will not be entertained.
  • The judging criteria for the task will be announced before the start of the round.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and undebatable.
