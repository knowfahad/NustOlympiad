Rules and Regulations
  • Singles/Doubles competitions will be arranged.
  • International table tennis Federation rules will be followed.
  • Umpire’s decision will be final and won’t be challenged.
  • Players would not be briefed about the rules before the game.
  • Players can be divided into pools (depending about participants).
  • Group stages matches can be best of three sets.
  • After completion of pool matches top players from each pool will advance to semifinals.
  • Final will be among the winners of Semifinals and will be best of 5 or 7 sets (depending on time availability and will be decided by the organizers and can’t be challenged).
  • Every participant must be in the court 10 minutes before the match.
  • Walkover will be given after 5 minutes of match timing.
  • Any participants found violating any rule inside the premises of NUST can be subjected to withdrawal from Tournament and accommodation.
  • Proper kits and non-marking shoes are recommended for all participants for all participants to play on synthetic mate inside NUST gymnasium.
Note
  • Rules are subjected to change
  • Event head has the right to change the rules and call disqualifications as per circumstances.
