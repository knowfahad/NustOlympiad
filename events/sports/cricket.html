Cricket

Rules and Regulations
  • Knockout format.
  • 11 players in each team.
  • 6 overs match only, 1 bowler can bowl only 2 over’s.
  • Disqualification in case of any unethical conduct.
  • Batsman will golden bowl will decide a golden bowl in each over.
  • On every golden bowl the score is counted twice.
  • Leg-byes and byes are not included.
  • Free-hit on any kind of No-ball.
  • If a team doesn’t show up after 15 minutes of start time, they’ll be disqualified.
