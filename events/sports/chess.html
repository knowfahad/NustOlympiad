Chess

Rules and Regulations
  • In all the games, each player will have 25 minutes. To decide which wins FIDE chess rules are applicable.
  • A player can win a game either by check mate on time or by resignation.
  • Touch-move rule will be applied.
  • For a game to be declared a tie, rules of stalemate, insufficient material, 3 fold repetitions and 50 moves draw rule are all applicable.
  • When a pawn reaches its eighth rank, the player has a choice to do queening or under promotion.
  • Outside assistance is not allowed.
  • In case of conflict, the jurisdiction of the organizer will be considered a final verdict.
  • If you are 10 min late for your game, organizer has the authority to give walkover.
